#### LLM guardrails -
* LLM guardrails are safety mechanisms and predefined rules that guide and constrain the behavior of large language models to ensure they operate within safe, ethical, and reliable boundaries.
* They work by screening user inputs, modifying or validating the model's outputs, and preventing harmful, biased, or inappropriate content, such as hate speech, misinformation, or privacy violations
